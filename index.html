About.

Jasbinder (fondly known as Jassi) started his creative journey in painting, in his school in Rourkela, under the guidance of his Guru (teacher) Jaspal Singh who first taught him how to hold the pencil and brush. Then followed Jyoti Bhatt and Vasudeven and Suresh (painters in Baroda College of Art) who inspired him with professionalism and refinement for the BFA Degree in Fine Arts, M.S.University of Baroda.
Jassi then did a three-year Diploma in Animation Filmmaking from Zed Institute of Creative Arts at Hyderbad. Victor Arcgansoves, his Russian animation teacher taught him how to makes lines dance while animating. I.S. Mathur and Valadmir Tarasov taught him film making at Hyderabad.
Last but not least Simi Nallaseth (Epiphany Films) a filmmaker believed in his work and together, with Simi, Jassi has developed designs and art-driection fore “I” (a rock opera film) and Dream Blanket (3D animated film).
Jassi has worked on films, music-videos and tv series. His main interest lies in feature films, short films and music-videos.
Clients : Zee Network, Grey Worldwide, UTV Motion Pictures, Epiphany Films as Designer and Art Director.
Jassi is teaching Art & Design, 2D and Stopmotion Animation in the school of animation at Whistlingwoods International, Mumbai since the last 6 years.
He paints regular in various medium but water color and acrylic are his favourite.
He believes that his soul is “Art”(Paintings, Digital Art, Installation art, Video Art etc) and his body is teaching, making and mentoring “Art in the Motion”.

Currently developing own Game Art and a 2D Animation short film.
