About.

- A Life-Long Learner -

Jasbinder (fondly known as jassi) started his creative journey in painting, from school in Rourkela, under the guidance of my Guru (teacher) Jaspal Singh who first taught him how to hold the pencil and brush. Then followed Jyoti Bhatt and many more (painters in Baroda College of Art) who inspired him with professionalism and refinement for the BFA Degree(Painting) in Fine Arts, M.S.University of Baroda.
show more Jassi then did a three-year Diploma in Animation Filmmaking from Zed Institute of Creative Arts at Hyderbad. Victor Arcgansoves, my Russian animation teacher taught him how to makes lines dance while animating. I.S. Mathur and Valadmir Tarasov taught him film making at Hyderabad. Last but not least Simi Nallaseth (Epiphany Films) a filmmaker believed in his work and together, with Simi, Jassi has developed designs and art-direction fore “I” (a rock opera film) and Dream Blanket (3D animated film). Jassi had worked on films, music-videos and tv series. His main interest lies in feature films, short films and music-videos. Clients : Zee Network, Grey Worldwide, UTV Motion Pictures, Epiphany Films as Designer and Art Director. Jassi is teaching Art & Design, 2D and Stopmotion Animation along with mentoring degree short films in the school of animation at Whistling Woods International, Mumbai since the last 6 years. He paints regular in various medium but water color and acrylic are his favourite.
He believes that my soul is “Art”(Paintings, Digital Art, Installation art, Video Art etc) and body is teaching, making and mentoring “Art in the Motion”.
Currently developing a animation short film and a Game Art Design. And keep participating group art shows.
